About

I am Muazzaz Ahmed Choudhury—known in creative circles as Faaiz Choudhury—an ardent student of law, currently pursuing my B.A. LL.B. with resolute passion and purpose. Originating from the culturally vibrant town of Karimganj in Assam, I carry with me not only the legacy of my roots but also a forward-looking vision shaped by justice, equity, and societal evolution.

My academic journey commenced at Tiny Hearts School and found firm grounding at Kendriya Vidyalaya, Karimganj, where I cultivated both discipline and curiosity—traits that continue to guide my legal pursuits.

Beyond the world of statutes and case law, I find expression in the realms of writing and music production. Through these creative avenues, I strive to distill truth, challenge convention, and spark reflection—blending intellect with artistry to give voice to both the seen and the unseen.
