About Me

I am Muazzaz Ahmed Choudhury, known also as Faaiz Choudhury, presently pursuing my B.A. LL.B. with earnest dedication. Hailing from the culturally rich town of Karimganj, Assam, I carry with me a deep-rooted sense of purpose and passion for law, justice, and societal growth. I did my kindergarten schooling from Tiny Hearts School and completed the rest of my foundational education from Kendriya Vidyalaya, Karimganj, Assam. With a flair for writing and a keen interest in music production, I continuously strive to express reality and provoke thought through my work.
